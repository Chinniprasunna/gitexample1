Customer Journey Map - Banking System Account Creation

1. Awareness

Goal: Discover the bank.

Touchpoints: Ads, website, referrals.

Emotions: Curious, interested.

2. Consideration

Goal: Explore account options.

Touchpoints: Website, customer support.

Emotions: Informed, evaluating.

3. Decision

Goal: Start account application.

Touchpoints: Online form, in-branch visit.

Emotions: Confident, focused.

4. Submission

Goal: Submit application.

Touchpoints: Confirmation page, email/SMS.

Emotions: Anticipation, uncertainty.

5. Activation

Goal: Account approval and use.

Touchpoints: Email/SMS, account dashboard.

Emotions: Excited, relieved.

6. Post-Activation

Goal: Regular account use.

Touchpoints: App, customer support.

Emotions: Satisfied, loyal.
